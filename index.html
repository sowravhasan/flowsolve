Our technology

FlowSolve.io's Zeptostill technology marks a significant breakthrough in merging water purification with Bitcoin mining, turning waste heat into valuable distilled water. Born from the realization that the heat from data centers poses both an environmental challenge and an opportunity, the Zeptostill leverages this byproduct to address the critical need for clean water.

This system uses the ideal gas equation and evaporative cooling from Bitcoin mining to distill water efficiently, controlled by a proprietary microcontroller for optimal purity and efficiency. Unlike traditional methods dependent on cooler climates, the Zeptostill excels in warmer conditions, enhancing thermal efficiency and water production capabilities.

This innovation embodies FlowSolve.io's commitment to dual-purpose technologies that promote environmental sustainability while solving practical problems, illustrating a major step forward in using advanced technology to benefit the planet.
